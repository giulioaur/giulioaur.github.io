Hello, it's a me, Giulio (or DrSushi for my online fellows), Italian who does not love espresso but does love pizza. My adventure began years ago, in a Christmas'Eve night, when my parents gifted me a big grey box that allowed me to play the story of a little purple dragon. From that moment on, I have always been a videogames lover, and when the moment of choosing my class specialization come, my decision was pretty simple to take: become a videogames programmer. Now I finally am the one that creates new amazing world and allows billions of people to live wonderful adventures. Now I am the one that add more PEW PEW to your game, and I do love it a lot!

Videogames are my main quest, but not my entire (open) world, I'm a base-pack nerd: tabletop games, d&d experience, tv-series, most famous anime and stuff like that. Furthermore the shadow of my Italian roots lords over me, usually pulling me down to a well full of love for every kind of cuisine and cooking. Craft Weisse beer, buying fancy costumes and cutie stuff are the three things I never say no to. But the Ring will always try to return to its Master, so you would eventually find me playing some online videogame, losing most of the time since I am definitely not a good player.
